Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your journey!

Why should I choose Malaysia over Europe or the US?

Malaysia offers top-ranked universities at a fraction of the cost. Living expenses are similar to India, flights are cheaper, and the‍healthcare system is affordable. You get international exposure without the high financial burden.

Will I get support after reaching Malaysia?

Yes, you’ll be part of a strong student community. From airport pickup to settling in, networking events, and ongoing support,‍we ensure you never feel alone in a new country.

How long does the visa process take, and do you help with it?

Absolutely. The visa process usually takes 4-6 weeks, and we handle all the paperwork to make sure you get approved smoothly.

Can I study at a globally recognized university in Malaysia?

Yes, Malaysia is home to top-ranked universities with internationally recognized degrees. Many Malaysian universities also have
partnerships with universities in the UK, Australia, and the US, giving you access to global opportunities.
